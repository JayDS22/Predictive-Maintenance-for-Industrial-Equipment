What this platform does

Industrial assets show sensor-visible drift long before they stop working. The platform ingests multi-channel telemetry, engineers degradation-aware features, and emits a Remaining Useful Life estimate plus a binary failure-risk score for every machine in a fleet.

  • End-to-end pipeline from raw telemetry to inference in under 100 ms.
  • Group-aware cross-validation by machine prevents unit-level leakage.
  • Cox PH and Kaplan-Meier in R for fleet-level reliability reporting.
  • ARIMA forecasting projects RUL 20 cycles ahead; optional LSTM hook.
  • Operator dashboard surfaces risk bands, sensor traces, recommended actions.

System architecture

Five stages, isolated and independently testable. Each box maps to a Python module or Flask endpoint in the repository.

NASA-style sensor CSV or live stream. Schema: 14 sensors plus 3 op-settings per cycle.
